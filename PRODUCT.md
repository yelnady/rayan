# Welcome to Rayan: Your Living Memory Palace

Rayan is an AI-powered assistant that transforms your life experiences into an immersive, navigable 3D world. Instead of searching through folders of notes or recordings, you can literally **walk through your memories** and talk to them.

---

## 🧠 How It Works: The Capture Agent

At the heart of Rayan is the **Capture Agent**. Think of it as your digital "ears and brain" that co-listens to your world and decides what's worth keeping.

### A Real-World Example
Imagine you're attending a Biology lecture about cellular energy. You click **"Start Capture"** in Rayan and share your screen/audio.

1.  **Passive Listening**: Rayan stays silent in the background, carefully listening to the professor's explanation.
2.  **Instant Extraction**: When the professor says, *"The Mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell,"* Rayan recognizes this as a high-value concept.
3.  **Real-Time Acknowledgment**: You'll hear Rayan briefly chime in: *"Got it – Mitochondria."* He has already summarized the point and stored it as a "Knowledge Artifact."
4.  **Interactive Help**: If you're confused, you can simply ask: *"Rayan, what does ATP stand for?"* He'll use his built-in tools (like web search) to give you a clear, spoken answer without stopping the capture.
5.  **Spatial Organizing**: Once the session ends, Rayan doesn't just save a text file. He creates a **Biology Room** in your 3D Palace and places the Mitochondria "artifact" on a pedestal for you to revisit later.

---

## 🏛️ Explore Your Palace

Your memories aren't just data—they are a place. Rayan builds a 3D environment (your "Memory Palace") where:

*   **Topics become Rooms**: Each subject you learn about gets its own dedicated space.
*   **Memories become Artifacts**: Key insights appear as floating holograms, framed images, or digital books.
*   **Intuitive Navigation**: Walk through your palace using your keyboard or mouse, just like a video game.

---

## 🗣️ Natural Conversations

You don't need to learn complex commands to interact with your palace.

*   **Voice Search**: Ask, *"Take me to my notes on Machine Learning,"* and Rayan will walk you to the right room.
*   **Deep Recall**: Click on an artifact and ask, *"Why was this important?"* Rayan will narrate its significance, drawing from the original session and even adding related details he found on the web.
*   **Interruptible**: Feel free to cut Rayan off with a follow-up question—he's designed for natural, back-and-forth dialogue.

---

## ✨ Automated Enrichment

Rayan doesn't just remember what *you* saw; he helps you understand it better. 
*   **Autonomous Research**: After you save a memory, Rayan's "Enrichment Agent" goes to work, searching the web for diagrams, related articles, and context to make your artifacts even more valuable.
*   **Visual Aids**: Rayan can generate diagrams and flowcharts on the fly to help explain complex topics during your conversations.

**Rayan is more than a note-taking app—it's a second home for your mind.**
